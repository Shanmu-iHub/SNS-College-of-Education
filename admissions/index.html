Admission Process

1

Eligibility Criteria

Candidates must have completed a 10+2+3(15) or 11+1+3(15) pattern of study. Candidates who have passed the UG/PG degree under the Open University system without a 10+2 pattern are not eligible.

2

Required Marks

  • OC: 50%
  • BC/BCM: 45%
  • MBC/DNC: 43%
  • SC/SCA/ST: 40%
3

Application

Fill out the admission and enquiry form below or visit the college office at Chinavedampatti for direct registration.

4

Selection

Selection is based on merit in accordance with the rules of the government of Tamil Nadu and the university regulations.
